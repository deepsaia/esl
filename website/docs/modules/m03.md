# Module 03: Population-Based ES

Why settle for one parent when you can have a whole population arguing about who's fittest? This module introduces comma and plus selection strategies, recombination operators, and self-adaptation - where the strategy learns to learn.

## Learning Objectives

1. Implement $(\\mu,\\lambda)$-ES and $(\\mu+\\lambda)$-ES from scratch.
2. Understand comma vs. plus selection and when each is preferable.
3. Implement intermediate and weighted recombination.
4. Build self-adaptive ES where mutation step sizes evolve alongside solutions.
5. Understand the role of population size, selection pressure, and recombination in convergence.
