# Module 02: The (1+1)-ES

Meet the simplest evolution strategy: one parent, one child, may the fittest survive. We'll build the (1+1)-ES from scratch and discover the 1/5th success rule — one of the earliest adaptive step-size rules and a surprisingly effective hack from 1973.

## Learning Objectives

1. Implement the (1+1)-ES with Gaussian mutation.
2. Understand and implement Rechenberg's 1/5th success rule for step-size adaptation.
3. Compare isotropic vs. per-coordinate mutation step sizes.
4. Analyze convergence behavior on unimodal and multimodal functions.
5. Trace the historical evolution from Rechenberg and Schwefel to modern ES.
