# Evolution Strategies — A Learning Journey

Welcome! This site is a hands-on, code-first guide to Evolution Strategies and evolutionary computation. Every concept comes with runnable Python, real-world context, and just enough math to know what's actually going on.

Whether you're here to understand CMA-ES, evolve a neural network, or watch a population of solutions swarm toward an optimum like ants finding sugar — you're in the right place.

## How to use this course

1. Start with [Module 00: Setup & Tools](/course/modules/m00) to get your evolutionary lab running.
2. Follow the modules in order — each one builds on the last.
3. Run the code. Break the code. Mutate the code. That's where learning happens.
4. Check the boxes on exercises as you complete them — your progress is saved in your browser.
